# Buddy Check-In Guide

## Overview
Organise a check-in with your buddy (15-30 minutes) to discuss questions/issues/progress and help each other implement the tasks of this week.

## What to Do

### Before the Check-In
- Write down questions or issues that came up during your work
- Document your progress on assigned tasks
- Prepare any code or materials you want to review together

### During the Check-In
- Share your progress with your buddy
- Discuss any blockers or challenges you've encountered
- Help each other troubleshoot issues
- Provide feedback and suggestions

### After the Check-In
- Bring questions/issues to the meeting session
- Add them directly to the **Miro board** under **"Meeting 2"** â†’ **"Questions this week"**

## Tips for Effective Check-Ins
- Be concise and focused (keep to 15-30 minutes)
- Come prepared with specific questions
- Share knowledge and solutions
- Note blockers for team discussion
- Document outcomes for reference

---

**Remember:** Regular check-ins help keep the team aligned and ensure everyone gets the support they need!
